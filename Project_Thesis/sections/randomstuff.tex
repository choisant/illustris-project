The density profile of a dark matter halo is found to fit the NFW-profile \parencite{Navarro1996},
\begin{equation}
    \frac{\rho}{\rho_{crit}} = \frac{\delta_c}{(r/r_c)(+1+r/r_s)^2.}
\end{equation}
